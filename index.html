Short Bio

Dr. Badihi received his Ph.D. degree in mechanical engineering from Concordia University, Montréal, Québec, Canada, in 2016. He was a Horizon Postdoctoral Fellow with Concordia University for two years. Since 2019, he has been an Associate Professor with the College of Automation Engineering, Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics, China.

His research interests are in all aspects of:

  • Condition monitoring, diagnostics, and prognostics

  • Fault-tolerant and cyber-attack resilient control

  • Fault-tolerant cooperative control.

Specific application areas of interest include:

  • Wind turbines and solar photovoltaic systems

  • Renewable microgrids and smart grid
